## You Are Not Static

One of the Enneagram's most powerful features is its dynamism. Each type is connected to two others by lines — arrows that describe how you shift under different conditions.

## Growth & Stress Directions

When you're **growing** — feeling secure, healthy, and expanding — you take on the positive qualities of your growth arrow type.

When you're **stressed** — feeling pressured, threatened, or reactive — you take on the less healthy qualities of your stress arrow type.

## How to Use This

- **Recognize your stress patterns.** When you notice yourself falling into your stress arrow behaviors, it's a signal — not a failure. It means something needs attention.
- **Lean into your growth direction.** Your growth arrow describes qualities that don't come naturally but are deeply rewarding when cultivated.
- **Don't oversimplify.** You don't "become" another type. You access certain qualities of that type — for better or worse.

## The Invitation

The arrows remind us that growth is possible and specific. You don't need generic self-help advice — you need the particular medicine your type calls for.
