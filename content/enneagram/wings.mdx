## Your Type, With a Twist

Your wing is one of the two types that sit next to your core type on the Enneagram circle. It doesn't change your fundamental type, it flavors it.

## How Wings Work

- A **Type 4** can have a **3 wing** (4w3) or a **5 wing** (4w5)
- A **Type 9** can have an **8 wing** (9w8) or a **1 wing** (9w1)
- You typically lean more strongly toward one wing, though both may be active at different times in your life

## What Changes

Your wing influences:
- Your **interpersonal style** (more outgoing or more withdrawn)
- Your **energy** (more assertive or more receptive)
- Your **focus** (blending your type's motivation with adjacent concerns)

## A Note of Caution

Don't rush to identify your wing. It's more important to understand your core type first. The wing adds nuance, but the core motivation is what matters most for self-understanding and growth.
