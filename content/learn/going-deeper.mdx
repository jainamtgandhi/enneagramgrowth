## Practices for Growth

### Daily Check-In
At the end of each day, ask yourself:
- When did my type pattern show up today?
- Was I operating from a healthy or reactive place?
- What would the growth direction of my type have looked like?

### Journaling Prompts
Use the reflection prompts at the end of each type page as journal starters. Write freely — don't edit or judge.

### Body Awareness
The Enneagram is not just a cognitive system. Pay attention to where you feel your type in your body — tension, openness, contraction, warmth.
